There are 2 bird books that I've read in the past few years. 1 was "Owls of the Eastern Ice," which was a bleak but eloquent account of a grad students time in Siberia studying fish owls. The other was "A Most Remarkable Creature", which is about Caracaras. The first half of that book was not very gripping, but the second half was a great travelogue of a person venturing into an unknown land. It wasn't bad - but if you were to read but one, I would recommend the former.
